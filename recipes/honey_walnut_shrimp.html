Honey Walnut Shrimp

Description

This is a simple honey walnut shrimp recipe. There is a 15 minute prep time and a 20 minute cook time. it will give about 4 servings.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup water
  • .66 cup white sugar
  • .5 cup walnuts
  • 4 large eggs
  • .66 cup mochiko
  • 1 cup vegetable oil for frying
  • 1 lb large shrimp, peeled and deveined
  • .25 cup mayonnaise
  • 2 tbps honey
  • 1 tbps canned sweetened condensed milk

Steps

  1. Stir together water and sugar in a small saucepan over high heat. Bring to a boil and add walnuts. Boil for 2 minutes, then drain and place walnuts on a cookie sheet to dry.
  2. Whip egg whites in a medium bowl until foamy. Stir in mochiko until it has a pasty consistency.
  3. Heat oil in a heavy deep skillet over medium-high heat.
  4. Dip shrimp in mochiko batter, then fry in batches in hot oil until golden brown, about 5 minutes. Remove with a slotted spoon and drain on paper towels.
  5. Stir in garlic and cook just until fragrant, about 1 minute. Stir in cucumber mixture; sprinkle with red pepper flakes. Set carrot mixture aside in a bowl.
  6. Stir together mayonnaise, honey, and sweetened condensed milk in a medium serving bowl. Add fried shrimp and toss to coat with sauce. Sprinkle candied walnuts on top and serve.
